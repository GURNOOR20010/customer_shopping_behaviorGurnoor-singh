# 🛍️ Customer Behavior Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Customer Behavior Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze customer purchasing patterns, subscription status, revenue generation, sales performance, and demographic trends. This dashboard helps businesses understand customer behavior and make data-driven decisions to improve customer engagement and sales performance.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze customer purchasing behavior.
- Track revenue and sales across product categories.
- Understand subscription trends among customers.
- Identify revenue and sales contributions by age group.
- Monitor customer satisfaction through review ratings.
- Support strategic business decision-making.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Highlights

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- **Number of Customers:** 3.9K
- **Average Purchase Amount:** $59.76
- **Average Review Rating:** 3.75

---

## 📈 Dashboard Insights

### Customer Subscription Analysis
- 73% of customers are not subscribed.
- 27% of customers have active subscriptions.

### Revenue by Category
- Clothing generates the highest revenue.
- Accessories contribute the second-highest revenue.
- Outerwear generates the lowest revenue.

### Sales by Category
- Clothing leads in total sales.
- Accessories follow closely behind.
- Footwear and Outerwear contribute smaller portions of sales.

### Revenue by Age Group
- Young Adults generate the highest revenue.
- Middle-aged customers are the second-largest contributors.
- Adults and Seniors contribute relatively similar revenue levels.

### Sales by Age Group
- Young Adults account for the highest sales volume.
- Middle-aged customers show strong purchasing activity.
- Seniors and Adults contribute moderate sales volumes.
